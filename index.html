Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is an iconic symbol of love built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Red Fort

The Red Fort in Delhi, made of red sandstone, served as the main residence of Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar, a UNESCO World Heritage site in Delhi, is a 73-meter tall minaret built in the early 13th century.
